Who are we?

Premier Football Coaching has been at the heart of Manchester since 2010; providing Children and teenagers aged between 9 and 18 years academy standard training and increased chances of progressing into a professional club. Founded by Vinnie Ryan and Leroy Jimmy, they had a goal to unearth talents, that would not be picked up by professional clubs, a second chance with their experience and high level qualifications. With over 50 players progressing to a professional club since being with us, could YOU be the next?
