Prices

A one off, 1 hour training session will cost £25 however if you book 3 sessions out of 5 this will cost £60 and if you book on all 5 session for your age group this will cost £75.
There is no joining fee, no monthly subscription and no commitment.

What happens after I make a booking?

After making a booking Vinnie Ryan will contact you within 24 hours to introduce himself and find out more about your current football ability, yours goals and what you hope to gain from the sessions. This is an important first step as it provides Vinnie an understanding of the players footballing experience, motivations and overall goals.

What happens if i cant make a session?

We understand that life gets in the way of things and that injuries and illness can occur at any time. Ideally Vinnie needs to be made aware upto 24 hours before so we can offer a full refund for that session or re-arrange the session with a new time and date.
Just contact us us for a refund or to rearrange a session.

The players safety

The safety of the player is of paramount importance, that's why all coaches undergo a rigorous recruitment process. Each coach is DBS certified, first aid and emergency aid trained.
