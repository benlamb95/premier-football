Meet our coaches!

Vinnie Ryan - Head Coach

A professional football coach for over 13 years, the Head Coach has worked with players from elite level football across the North West of England, across the USA, Sao Paulo, Brazil and Accra, Ghana. He holds the UEFA A Licence and the Advanced Youth Award, the highest qualification in football coaching. He has spent 7 years working with the Foundation Phases at Burnley FC Academy and the Youth Development Phase at Oldham Athletic FC. He is passionate about coaching local youth players and has enjoyed seeing many progress and sign to Premier League and Football League clubs.
