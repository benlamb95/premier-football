Meet our coaches!

Vinny Ryan

Head Coach

Age:43


"Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work!"

A professional football coach for over 13 years, the Head Coach has worked with players from elite level football across the North West of England, across the USA, Sao Paulo, Brazil and Accra, Ghana.
He holds the UEFA A Licence and the Advanced Youth Award, the highest qualification in football coaching.
He has spent 7 years working with the Foundation Phases at Burnley FC Academy and the Youth Development Phase at Oldham Athletic FC.
He is passionate about coaching local youth players and has enjoyed seeing many progress and sign to Premier League and Football League clubs.

Leroy Jimmy

- Head of Fitness & Science

Age:40


Good players inspire themselves
Great players inspire others

Leroy began his professional career playing for Rochdale and then moving to Bolton where he played 300 games in the first team as centre back.
Leroy graduated from Loughborough University with a degree in sports science and he completed a master's in leadership and emotional intelligence.
He holds the UEFA A license but specialises in strength and conditioning and sports psycholigists. Helping you to get your body and mind right.
He spent 5 years working at Leeds United Sports Science Department.

Haris Tom

- Set piece Specialist

Age:35


Football is a team game.
So is life.

Haris began his professional career at Arsenal however played most of his career at Sheffield United before injuries cut his career short.
He holds the UEFA A license and has prevously worked at Blackpool FC, Reading Fc and most recently Burnley where he met Vinny.
Haris is an expert at set peice coaching both attacking and defending.
Haris enjoys giving Children the chance to make it pro where it may not have been possible.
